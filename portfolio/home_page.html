Welcome

Welcome to the Portfolio Carrier by Noah Carrier this is a complete portfolio filled with all of my created works and accomplishments as a Computer Science major and also some of my personal projects that I have done over the years. I wanted all of my works to be easily found in a singular location and viewed as proof of my educational knowledge and background. The goal and objective for Portfolio Carrier is to be an intuitive way to show my qualifications as a web developer and a computer software engineer. I am excited to welcome you here and hope that it is a place where you find what you are looking for. I am currently open to future employment and anyone who is interested in some freelance work. Take some time and have a look around and see for yourself, I’m excited to get to know you and hope to be of service to your goals and desires. This Portfolio is going to be a Website that will be growing and updated regularly to make it more and more usable and better advanced for your enjoyment. I am happy to see you here and I encourage you to take a look around.
